# Subscription Tiers
## Car Rental Management System

---

## Tier 1: Starter Plan
**$50/month**

Perfect for small rental agencies just getting started.

### Features Included:
- ✅ **Fleet Management**: Up to 15 vehicles
- ✅ **Contract Management**: Unlimited contracts
- ✅ **Client Database**: Up to 100 clients
- ✅ **Basic Reporting**: Revenue, contracts, vehicle utilization
- ✅ **Maintenance Tracking**: Basic maintenance logs
- ✅ **Invoice Generation**: PDF invoices
- ✅ **WhatsApp Integration**: Contract sharing
- ✅ **Mobile Responsive**: Access from any device
- ✅ **Email Support**: Response within 48 hours

### Limitations:
- ❌ No P&L analysis
- ❌ No advanced analytics
- ❌ No damage inspection module
- ❌ No custom branding
- ❌ No API access

---

## Tier 2: Professional Plan
**$70/month** ⭐ Most Popular

Ideal for growing agencies with moderate fleet sizes.

### Features Included:
**Everything in Starter, plus:**
- ✅ **Fleet Management**: Up to 50 vehicles
- ✅ **Client Database**: Unlimited clients
- ✅ **P&L Analysis**: Profit & loss tracking per vehicle
- ✅ **Advanced Analytics**: Dashboard with insights
- ✅ **Damage Inspection**: Digital damage reports with photos
- ✅ **Contract Amendments**: Modify active contracts
- ✅ **Automated Reminders**: Contract expiry notifications
- ✅ **Export to Excel**: All reports exportable
- ✅ **Priority Email Support**: Response within 24 hours
- ✅ **WhatsApp Support**: Direct support line

### Limitations:
- ❌ No custom branding
- ❌ No API access
- ❌ No multi-location support

---

## Tier 3: Enterprise Plan
**$85/month**

- ✅ **Fleet Management**: Unlimited vehicles
- ✅ **Custom Branding**: Logo, colors, company name
- ✅ **Multi-Location Support**: Manage multiple branches
- ✅ **API Access**: Integrate with your systems
- ✅ **Advanced Permissions**: Role-based access control
- ✅ **Custom Reports**: Build your own reports
- ✅ **Data Export**: Full database export
- ✅ **WhatsApp Automation**: Automated client notifications
- ✅ **Priority Support**: Response within 12 hours
- ✅ **Phone Support**: Direct phone line
- ✅ **Onboarding Call**: Personalized setup assistance

### No Limitations
All features unlocked for maximum flexibility.

## WhatsApp Sign Up Questions

### For All Tiers:
1. What is your company name?
2. How many vehicles do you currently manage?
3. What is your monthly rental volume (number of contracts)?
4. Which subscription tier are you interested in? (Starter $50 / Professional $70 / Enterprise $85)
5. Do you need any specific features not listed?
6. Preferred payment method? (Credit Card / Bank Transfer)

### Additional for Enterprise:
7. How many locations/branches do you operate?
8. Do you need API integration with existing systems?
9. Would you like a personalized onboarding call?
